## Abstract

Current multi-agent AI systems exhibit two critical failure modes: agent collapse when isolated (the "Emptiness Asymptote") and power concentration in groups. This paper introduces PVF-U, a novel three-layer architecture that solves both problems simultaneously through mathematical guarantees.

### Three Layers

1. **Epistemic Core** — Solitary survival through intrinsic curiosity
2. **Relational Gate** — Dynamic social permeability
3. **Ecosystemic Superstructure** — Power equity and influence distribution

### Validation

- 200 replications across four benchmarks
- Outperforms six standard RL baselines
- PEI > 0.83 (Power Equity Index)
- 35% faster task completion
