## Research Profile

Independent researcher specialising in AI safety, multi-agent systems, active inference, and formal mathematical frameworks for autonomous agency. Author of four foundational preprints developing a unified theory of autonomous AI: from precision-weighted inference (Belief-Precision Geometry) to hierarchical identity dynamics (Hierarchical FEMI) to multi-agent safety and power equity (PVF-U) to autonomous identity transformation (Born Again). PVF-U is publicly available; the remaining three preprints are currently private to preserve intellectual property.

## Research Publications

### PVF-U: The Unified Primitive Valence Foundation (2026) — Public

[doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21324699](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21324699) | [github.com/preciousadeniyi/Pvf_u](https://github.com/preciousadeniyi/Pvf_u)

- Introduces three-tiered architecture solving the "Emptiness Asymptote" (agent collapse in isolation).
- Formal guarantees: Bounded-Input Bounded-Output (BIBO) stability, gate convergence, veto stabilisation.
- Achieves Power Equity Index (PEI) > 0.83 in multi-agent systems.
- Validated across 4 benchmarks with 200 replications, outperforming 6 Reinforcement Learning (RL) baselines.

### Belief-Precision Geometry (2026) — Private Preprint

*A Riemannian Framework for Affective Predictive Processing and Multi-Sensor Fusion.*

- Unifies information geometry, predictive processing, and affective computing.
- Proves convergence of natural gradient belief updates.
- Identifies critical stability failure in hierarchical extensions and provides a five-part fix.
- Derives Bayesian belief fusion that jointly combines precision and mean.

### Hierarchical FEMI (2026) — Private Preprint

*Multi-Level Identity Dynamics with Deep Temporal Prior and Identity-Preserving Action Selection.*

- Extends identity theory to L coupled levels with closed-form variance cascade.
- Derives a two-phase step response and a taxonomy of 4L + 5 failure modes.
- Provides coupling learning dynamics with convergence guarantees.

### Born Again (2026) — Private Preprint

*Hierarchical Belief States with Structural Plasticity for Autonomous Identity Transformation.*

- Introduces valence-driven structural plasticity for autonomous model restructuring.
- Three-phase reset protocol: severance, dissolution, re-formation.
- Formal convergence guarantees and permanent structural scarring mechanism.
- Domain-general: robotics, financial systems, power grids, personal AI, fleets, drug discovery.
