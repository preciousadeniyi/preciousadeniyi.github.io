## About Me

I am an independent AI safety researcher based in Oyo, Nigeria. I built the PVF-U framework — a novel three-layer architecture that guarantees solitary survival, dynamic social gating, and equitable multi-agent power distribution.

My work addresses two critical failure modes in multi-agent AI systems:
- **Agent collapse when isolated** (the "Emptiness Asymptote")
- **Power concentration in groups**

I am open-sourcing my work to ensure these safety mechanisms are globally accessible.
